1. Internal Sponsorships (FAST-NUCES Provided)
The university offers several forms of direct financial support:
Merit Scholarship for Bachelors: Awarded to the Top Three position holders of each Examination Board in their year of admission.
Semester-Wise Merit Awards: Cash prizes and certificates are given to undergraduate students who rank in the top three of their department each semester.
Eligibility: Students must have a regular course load and an SGPA of 3.00 or higher.
Study Loans: Interest-free loans are arranged for bright students who cannot afford the fees.
Rules for Internal Sponsorships
Coverage: Limited to tuition fees only.
Duration:
Bachelors: Maximum of 8 semesters.
Masters: Maximum of 4 semesters.
PhD: Maximum of 6 semesters.
Restrictions: Cannot be used for repeat or withdrawn courses; it only covers a course once.
Academic Standing: Sponsorship is suspended if a student's CGPA falls into the warning zone but is restored once the CGPA improves.

3. Need-Based & Community Assistance
OSAF & FANA: Financial assistance provided by the Old Students Association of FAST and the FAST Alumni of North America.
Ihsan Trust: Offers financial assistance for students unable to pay full fees.
General Refaqat Memorial: Specifically for students facing financial hardship.
Community-Based: Various foundations including Memon, Bohra, and Agha Khan Foundation offer specialized scholarships.
