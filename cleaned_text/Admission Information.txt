Admission Information

Admission to the university is strictly based on merit. There are no quotas, reserved seats, or admissions through donation. There is also no age limit for applicants.
Merit is determined through either:
The university’s own admission test, or
An equivalent test conducted by a recognized organization

Foreign Nationals

At the time of admission, foreign nationals must submit a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the Ministry of Federal Education and Professional Training.
Applicants can apply using the following tests:
NTS-NAT (within last 6 months) → Bachelor programs
GAT Subject → MS, MBA, PhD programs
SAT → Bachelor programs
GRE (General) → MS, MBA programs
GRE (Subject) → PhD programs

Candidates Awaiting Results

Students awaiting FA/FSc/A-Level results may apply.
Admission will be provisional
Must pass required exams to confirm admission
If failed → admission is cancelled
Tuition fee refunded as per HEC policy
Students must:
Inform the university immediately after results
Submit refund application if needed

Admission Procedure

Fill the application form online at: admissions.nu.edu.pk
A unique Application Reference Number (ARN) is generated
Pay application fee: Rs. 3000
Additional campus choice: Rs. 500 per campus
Additional test type: Rs. 500 per test
Deposit fee in designated bank (non-refundable)
Confirm all entered information is correct
Print Admit Card after payment
Bring admit card to test center (mandatory)

NU Online Admission Test (NU-OAT)

Conducted on computer
Candidate must know basic mouse/keyboard usage
Each candidate receives a different test
Sections appear in random order
Questions also appear randomly

Rules:

Can move within a section and change answers
Must arrive on time (late entry not allowed)
Bring only Admit Card
Test held at campuses:
Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Peshawar, Multan, Chiniot-Faisalabad
