1. Attendance Requirements
Ideal Requirement: Students should maintain 100% attendance in lectures, labs, and tutorials.
Minimum Threshold (Undergraduate): 80%.
Minimum Threshold (Graduate/PhD): 75%.
Condonation: Up to 20% of total lectures can be condoned for genuine reasons like illness.
Penalty: Failure to meet these requirements results in an F/A (Fail due to Attendance) grade and ineligibility for final exams.

2. Academic Grading System
Performance is typically split 50% for semester work (midterms, quizzes, etc.) and 50% for final examinations.
Letter Grades & Points
Special Grades:
W: Withdrawn (No GPA impact).
I: Incomplete (Converts to F if not completed by next semester).

3. Honors & Recognition
Rector’s List: SGPA of 4.00 (Undergraduates only).
Dean’s List: SGPA between 3.50 and 3.99.
Graduation Honors:
Summa Cum Laude: CGPA 3.90 – 4.00.
Magna Cum Laude: CGPA 3.75 – 3.89.
Cum Laude: CGPA 3.60 – 3.74.

4. Academic Warning & Probation
Warnings are issued if CGPA falls below:
BS/BBA: 2.00.
MS/MBA: 2.50.
PhD: 3.00.
Rule of Three: If the Warning Count reaches three, the student’s admission is closed.

5. Course Rules (FYP & Summer)
Final Year Project (FYP): To register for FYP-I, a student must have passed all courses from the first six semesters (deficiency of 1-2 courses may be allowed by HOD/Director).
Summer Semester: An optional 8–9 week session. Maximum of 2 courses allowed. Fees are non-refundable.

7. Disciplinary Action
Academic Dishonesty: Minimum punishment is zero marks in the relevant task; maximum is an 'F' grade in the course or expulsion.
Medium of Instruction: English is the standard for all exams, except for Religious/Islamic studies which can be answered in Urdu.
