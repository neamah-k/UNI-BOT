1. One-Time Admission Charges

Admission Fee: Rs. 30,000 (payable once at the time of admission).
Note: Graduates of FAST-NUCES admitted to a higher degree program do not pay this again.
Security Deposit: Rs. 20,000 (refundable upon degree completion or closing of admission).
Applicant Admission Fee: Rs. 3,000.

2. Recurring Semester Fees
Tuition Fee (All Programs/Campuses): Rs. 11,000 per credit hour.
Student Activities Fund: Rs. 2,500 per semester.
Late Payment Fine: Charged at a rate of Re 1 per day for every thousand rupees of the total outstanding balance.
