2. Hostel Facilities
On-campus housing is available at specific campuses with varying capacities and amenities.
Chiniot-Faisalabad Campus
Capacity: Accommodates over 800 students and faculty in separate hostels for boys and girls.
Amenities: Well-equipped mess, study room, food storage (freezer/oven), internet, iron facility, medical center, and a gymnasium/fitness center.
Recreation: Table tennis and billiard boards.
Peshawar Campus
On-Campus (Boys Only): Capacity for 150 students.
Room Features: Each student is provided with a bed, bedside table, study table, and a lockable cupboard.
General Facilities: Laundry, ironing, campus internet, and weekly pick/drop to city bus terminals on weekends.
External Housing: Campus-managed hostels are also available within walking distance for additional capacity.
